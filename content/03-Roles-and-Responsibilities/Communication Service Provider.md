# Communication Service Provider

## Role in PBCS
The Communication Service Provider provides communication service capability and performance evidence under contract/service agreements, including outage notification and network behaviour relevant to RCP.

## Typical evidence responsibilities
- Identify applicable RCP/RSP requirements.
- Provide or consume compliance evidence.
- Support operational approval or acceptance where applicable.
- Participate in post-implementation monitoring and corrective action.

## Source basis
- Doc 9869 distributes PBCS responsibilities across State authorities, ANSPs, aircraft operators, manufacturers, avionics suppliers, and communication/surveillance service providers.
- This note summarizes the practical role of this stakeholder in implementation, compliance, and monitoring.
