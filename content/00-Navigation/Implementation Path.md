## Common implementation mistakes

> [!warning] Mistake 1: Picking the spec before the operation
> Don't say "we'll use RCP 240/D" before defining what the ATM operation actually needs. The operation drives the spec, not the other way around.

> [!warning] Mistake 2: Treating responsibility as implied
> If the matrix doesn't have a specific person or team name in each cell, assume no one owns it yet. "The State will handle it" is not a plan.

> [!warning] Mistake 3: Deferring monitoring setup
> "We'll set up monitoring after we're operational" is the most common cause of compliance gaps. Design your monitoring programme during the approval phase.

> [!warning] Mistake 4: No service provider contracts before approval
> Your CSP/SSP must agree to provide performance and outage data. If this isn't in your contract, you can't run monitoring.
