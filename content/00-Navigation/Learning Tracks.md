# Learning Tracks

Different people need different things from PBCS. Choose the track that matches your role.

---

## 🎓 Track 1: Regulator / State Authority

**What you care about:** Policy, oversight, approvals, and what to prescribe in your AIP/SUPP.

| Step | Read | Time |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [[Beginner Path]] — the big picture | 15 min |
| 2 | [[State Authority]] — your specific role | 5 min |
| 3 | [[Developing RCP RSP Specifications]] — what to prescribe | 5 min |
| 4 | [[Applying RCP RSP Specifications]] — how to apply | 5 min |
| 5 | [[Compliance Path]] — the full approval flow | 10 min |
| 6 | [[Operational Approval]] — what you need to check | 10 min |
| 7 | [[PBCS Compliance Matrix Template]] — your audit tool | 5 min |

**Total:** ~55 minutes
