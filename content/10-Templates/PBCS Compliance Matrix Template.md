# PBCS Compliance Matrix Template

Copy this into a working implementation file. Replace generic rows with the actual State/regional requirement, operation, aircraft/operator, ANSP, CSP/SSP, and monitoring evidence.

| Compliance item | Doc 9869 source route | Applicable actor | Evidence to attach | Status | Owner | Review date |

## Boundary
This template is a source-routed checklist, not evidence itself. A row is not compliant until the actual evidence is attached and accepted by the relevant authority or company owner.
