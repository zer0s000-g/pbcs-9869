# Open Questions

- Which State/regional PBCS requirements apply to the company's intended operations?
- Which RCP/RSP specifications are actually prescribed for the relevant routes/airspace?
- Which company aircraft/operators already have operational approval evidence?
- Which CSP/SSP contracts provide outage/performance data suitable for monitoring?
- Who owns continued compliance review and corrective-action closure?
