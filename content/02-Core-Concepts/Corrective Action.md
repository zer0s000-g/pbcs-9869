# Corrective Action

## Short definition
Corrective action is the response to observed degraded performance, reported problems, or non-compliant operations. It can involve analysis, stakeholder coordination, service-provider action, operational mitigation, or approval/compliance follow-up.

## Operational meaning
This concept should be interpreted inside the full PBCS lifecycle: specification development, application to an ATM operation, initial compliance, operational approval where applicable, and post-implementation monitoring.
