# Flight Plan Requirements

## Short definition
Doc 9869 links compliance with RCP/RSP specifications to flight plan requirements so that capabilities are visible to ATM systems and operational approval can be applied consistently.

## Operational meaning
This concept should be interpreted inside the full PBCS lifecycle: specification development, application to an ATM operation, initial compliance, operational approval where applicable, and post-implementation monitoring.
