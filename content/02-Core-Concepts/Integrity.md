# Integrity

## Short definition
Integrity addresses confidence that information is not undetectedly corrupted or manipulated in a way that compromises operational safety. Doc 9869 allocates integrity provisions across end systems and networks.

## Operational meaning
This concept should be interpreted inside the full PBCS lifecycle: specification development, application to an ATM operation, initial compliance, operational approval where applicable, and post-implementation monitoring.
