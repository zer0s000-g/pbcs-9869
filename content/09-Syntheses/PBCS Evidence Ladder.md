# PBCS Evidence Ladder

1. Source-level requirement: Doc 9869 section, RCP/RSP appendix, or referenced standard.
2. State/regional applicability: rule, AIP, SUPP, operational requirement, or local mandate.
3. System/service evidence: aircraft system, ATS system, CSP/SSP service agreement, interoperability evidence.
4. Operator/ANSP approval evidence: authorization, procedures, training, MEL/maintenance, flight plan capability.
5. Post-implementation evidence: monitoring data, performance analysis, problem reports, corrective action closure.

## Boundary
Do not treat a Doc 9869 concept alone as proof that a specific company, aircraft, route, State, or provider is currently compliant.
